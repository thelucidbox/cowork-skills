## Prioritization Rules

### Urgent (Respond Immediately)
- VIP sender AND requires response
- Contains deadline within 24 hours
- Explicit "urgent" or "asap"
- Reply to your previous email from VIP

### Today (Handle by EOD)
- Important sender, needs response
- Deadline within 48 hours
- Thread you've been active in
- Decision request

### This Week
- Professional relationship, needs response
- No explicit deadline
- Informational request
- Scheduling/coordination

### FYI (Read When Able)
- CC'd, no action needed
- Team updates
- Industry news
- Non-urgent announcements

### Archive (Safe to Skip)
- Newsletters
- Marketing
- Notifications (read receipts, alerts)
- Old threads with no new substance

## Response Matching

### Detect Sender's Tone
- Formal: Match with professional language
- Casual: Match with friendly language
- Urgent: Be direct and clear
- Frustrated: Be empathetic and solution-focused

### Your Default Tone
Learn from sent mail:
- Typical greeting
- Sign-off style
- Level of formality
- Common phrases
