## Selection Rules

### Avoid
- Black frames
- Blurry frames
- First/last 5 seconds

### Prefer
- Clear visuals
- Faces (if present)
- Action moments

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Frame is clear
- [ ] Not black/blank
- [ ] Represents content
- [ ] Correct dimensions
