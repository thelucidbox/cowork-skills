# Questions Log

> Track questions asked during development, who initiated them, and how they were resolved.
> This becomes decision archaeology - understanding why we made the choices we did.

## Template

```
### Q: [Question]
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Asked by:** Fabian | Claude | Other
- **Context:** Why this question came up
- **Resolution:** How it was answered/decided
- **Impact:** What changed as a result
```
