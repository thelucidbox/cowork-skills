# Future Meeting Brief Template

## Purpose

This file captures context to make future meetings with these attendees more effective. Reference it before your next meeting with this group.

## Structure

```markdown
# Meeting Brief: [Group/Person Name]

**Last Meeting:** [Date]
**Next Expected:** [Date or "TBD"]

---

## Key Context

### Relationship Status
- [One line: where things stand]

### Recent Decisions
- [Decision 1] — [Date]
- [Decision 2] — [Date]

### Open Items Carrying Forward
- [Item needing follow-up]
- [Question still unresolved]

---

## People Notes

### [Person 1 Name]
- **Role**: [Title/function]
- **Communication style**: [Brief/detailed, formal/casual]
- **Hot buttons**: [Topics they care about]
- **Notes**: [Anything useful for next interaction]

### [Person 2 Name]
...

---

## Meeting Patterns

- **Preferred format**: [Video/phone/in-person]
- **Typical length**: [30 min/60 min]
- **Best scheduling**: [Morning/afternoon, days to avoid]
- **Agenda preference**: [Sent ahead/discussed live]

---

## Topics for Next Meeting

- [ ] [Topic from open items]
- [ ] [Follow-up from decisions]
- [ ] [Relationship maintenance item]

---

## History

| Date | Type | Key Outcome |
|------|------|-------------|
| [Date] | [Meeting type] | [One-line outcome] |

---

*Updated: [Date of last debrief]*
```

## Usage Notes

- Update after each meeting with this group
- Reference before scheduling or preparing
- Accumulates over time — don't delete history
- Flag if relationship needs attention

## Auto-Population

When calendar and email integration enabled:
- Last meeting date pulled from calendar
- Communication style inferred from email patterns
- History built from previous debriefs
