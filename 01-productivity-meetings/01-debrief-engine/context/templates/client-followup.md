```

**Outstanding Items:**
- [Any items needing client input]

[Professional sign-off],
[Your name]
[Your title]
```

## Tone Notes

- Open with appreciation
- Use "we" language for collaborative feel
- Be specific about who owns what
- Close with invitation for correction

## Length Target

200-400 words. Complete but scannable.

## Customization Points

- `[Client Name]`: First name if relationship established, otherwise Mr./Ms.
- `[Meeting Topic]`: Specific topic, not just "our meeting"
- `[Professional sign-off]`: "Best regards" / "Looking forward to next steps" / "Warm regards"

## What NOT to Include

- Internal disagreements
- Budget discussions unless confirmed
- Tentative items presented as final
- Anything marked confidential/internal
