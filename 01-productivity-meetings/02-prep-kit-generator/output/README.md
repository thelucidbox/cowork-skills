# Prep Kit Generator — Output Folder

## What Appears Here

After running prep kit generator:

```
output/
├── attendee-profiles.md      # Background on all attendees
├── talking-points.md         # Topics to cover and questions
├── draft-agenda.md           # Proposed meeting structure
└── post-meeting-template.md  # Ready for your notes
```

---

## File Descriptions

### attendee-profiles.md
Research on each meeting attendee:
- Professional background
- Company context
- Your relationship history
- Conversation starters

### talking-points.md
Structured preparation:
- Must-cover topics
- Questions to ask
- Questions to expect (with suggested responses)
- Topics to avoid

### draft-agenda.md
Proposed meeting structure:
- Timed agenda
- Topic owners
- Desired outcomes
- Pre-meeting checklist

### post-meeting-template.md
Ready-to-use note template:
- Sections for decisions, actions, questions
- Follow-up checklist
- Space for raw notes

---

## Using the Outputs

### Before the Meeting
1. Review `attendee-profiles.md` — know who you're meeting
2. Customize `talking-points.md` — add your specifics
3. Share `draft-agenda.md` with attendees (optional)
4. Print/open `post-meeting-template.md` for notes

### During the Meeting
- Use `post-meeting-template.md` for structured notes

### After the Meeting
- Feed notes to Debrief Engine for processing
- Save `attendee-profiles.md` for future reference

---

## Customization

Outputs follow templates in `context/templates/`. Modify to change:
- Profile depth and sections
- Talking point categories
- Agenda format
- Note-taking structure
