# Attendee Profile Template

## Structure

```markdown
## [Full Name]

**Role**: [Title] at [Company]
**LinkedIn**: [URL]
**Email**: [if known]

---

### Professional Background
- **Current**: [Current role, tenure, responsibilities]
- **Previous**: [1-2 relevant prior roles]
- **Education**: [If relevant to conversation]
- **Expertise**: [Key skills/domains]

---

### Company Context
- **Company**: [Name]
- **Industry**: [Industry]
- **Size**: [Employees, stage]
- **Recent News**: [1-2 relevant items]

---

### Your Relationship
- **First contact**: [Date or "This meeting"]
- **Previous meetings**: [Count and dates]
- **Last interaction**: [Date and context]
- **Email history**: [Brief summary]
- **Status**: [New / Active / Dormant / VIP]

---

### Communication Style
- **Observed**: [Direct/detailed, formal/casual]
- **Preferences**: [Email/call/text, time of day]
- **Notes**: [Any relevant observations]

---

### Conversation Hooks
- [Professional interest or recent achievement]
- [Mutual connection: Name, relationship]
- [Shared experience or interest]

---

### Meeting-Specific Notes
- **Their likely goals**: [What they want from meeting]
- **Your goals with them**: [What you want]
- **Potential concerns**: [What they might push back on]
```

## Usage Notes

- Profile depth scales with meeting importance
- First meetings: focus on research
- Repeat meetings: focus on history and evolution
- Flag any gaps clearly for user awareness
