## Tracking Rules

### Interaction Types
- **Email**: Sent or received (meaningful content, not newsletters)
- **Meeting**: Calendar event with person
- **Call**: Phone/video call
- **In-person**: Noted manually
- **Other**: Messages, social interactions

### What Counts as Interaction
- Substantive email exchange (not one-word replies)
- Meeting attendance
- Direct phone/video call
- In-person meeting

### What Doesn't Count
- CC'd on email
- Group newsletter
- Mass meeting (unless directly interacted)
- Social media likes (unless specifically noted)

---

## Health Classification

### Healthy
- VIP: Contact within 14 days
- Key: Contact within 30 days
- Active: Contact within 60 days
- Casual: Contact within 180 days

### Needs Attention
- Beyond normal but not critical
- Typically 1.5-2x normal frequency

### At Risk
- VIP: 30+ days
- Key: 60+ days
- Active: 120+ days
- Casual: 365+ days

## Reconnection Draft Rules

### Tone
- Warm but not apologetic
- Reference last interaction if natural
- Provide easy response hook

### Template Structure
```markdown
Subject: [Casual relevant subject]

Hi [Name],

[Warm opener, not apologetic about gap]

[Value/reason for reaching out]

[Easy question or update to respond to]

[Sign-off]
```

### Context Personalization
- Mention something from their profile
- Reference shared experience
- Include relevant update from your side
