## Output Format

### bibliography.md
```markdown
# Bibliography

## Articles
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, Volume(Issue), pages. DOI

## Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book*. Publisher.

## Web Sources
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. URL
```

### source-notes.md
```markdown
# Source Notes

## [Source 1 Title]
- **Citation**: [formatted citation]
- **Relevance**: High/Medium/Low
- **Key Points**:
  - Point 1
  - Point 2
- **Quotes**:
  > "Notable quote" (p. X)
- **Notes**: [Your annotations]
```

---

## Citation Rules

### Required Fields
- Author(s)
- Year/Date
- Title
- Source/Publisher
- Access information (URL, DOI)

### When Missing
- "n.d." for no date
- "Anonymous" if no author
- Note incomplete citation
