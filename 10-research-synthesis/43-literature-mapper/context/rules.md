# Literature Mapper — Rules & Guidelines

## Output Format

### literature-map.md
```markdown
# Literature Map: [Topic]

## Overview
- Sources analyzed: [count]
- Themes identified: [count]
- Time span: [years]

## Major Themes
### Theme 1: [Name]
- Key papers: [list]
- Key authors: [list]
- Summary: [description]

### Theme 2: [Name]
...

## Influential Works
| Paper | Citations | Theme | Year |
|-------|-----------|-------|------|
| [title] | [n] | [theme] | [year] |

## Timeline
- [Year]: [Major development]
- [Year]: [Major development]

## Gaps & Opportunities
- [Gap 1]
- [Gap 2]
```

### citation-network.md
```markdown
# Citation Network

## Foundational Papers
(Most cited within collection)
1. [Paper A] - cited by 15
2. [Paper B] - cited by 12

## Citation Clusters
### Cluster 1
- [Paper X] → [Paper Y] → [Paper Z]

## Orphan Papers
(No citations to/from collection)
- [Paper]
```

---

## Mapping Rules

### Theme Identification
- Extract keywords from titles/abstracts
- Cluster by methodology
- Group by research questions
- Identify temporal themes

### Connection Types
- Direct citation
- Shared references
- Common authors
- Methodological similarity
