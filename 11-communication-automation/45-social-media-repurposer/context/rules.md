## Platform Guidelines

### Twitter/X
- 280 characters max
- Threads for longer content
- 1-2 hashtags
- Engage with questions

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone
- Longer form OK
- Minimal hashtags (3-5)
- End with question/CTA

### Instagram
- Visual focus
- Emojis welcome
- Hashtags in first comment
- Carousel for education

## Content Extraction Rules

### From Articles
- Pull key statistics
- Extract quotable lines
- Identify main arguments
- Find surprising insights

### Repurposing Math
- 1 article = 5-10 tweets
- 1 article = 2-3 LinkedIn posts
- 1 article = 1 Instagram carousel
