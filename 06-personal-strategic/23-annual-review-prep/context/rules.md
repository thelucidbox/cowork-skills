# Annual Review Prep — Rules & Guidelines

## Output Formatting Rules

### self-review-draft.md Format
```markdown
# Self-Review: [Year]

**Name**: [Your name]
**Role**: [Title]
**Period**: [Date range]
**Manager**: [Name]

---

## Summary

[2-3 paragraph summary of the year—key themes, growth, impact]

---

## Key Accomplishments

### [Theme 1: e.g., Technical Delivery]

**[Accomplishment Title]**
- **Context**: [Situation/challenge]
- **Action**: [What you did]
- **Result**: [Impact, ideally quantified]

**[Accomplishment Title]**
...

### [Theme 2: e.g., Leadership]
...

---

## Goals Assessment

| Goal | Target | Actual | Status |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| [Goal 1] | [Target] | [Result] | Met/Exceeded/Partial |

---

## Growth & Learning

### Skills Developed
- [Skill]: [How demonstrated]

### Challenges Overcome
- [Challenge]: [How handled]

---

## Looking Ahead

### Goals for Next Year
- [Goal 1]
- [Goal 2]

### Development Areas
- [Area for growth]
```

## Accomplishment Identification

### Look For
- Projects completed/shipped
- Processes created or improved
- People helped (mentoring, support)
- Problems solved
- Initiatives led
- Recognition received
- Skills learned
- Goals achieved

### Quantify Where Possible
- Time saved
- Revenue influenced
- Errors prevented
- People reached
- Efficiency gained
- Cost reduced

---

## Theme Categories

### Technical/Delivery
- Features shipped
- Bugs fixed
- Systems built
- Technical decisions

### Leadership
- People managed
- Initiatives led
- Decisions influenced
- Culture shaped

### Collaboration
- Cross-team work
- Stakeholder management
- Communication

### Growth
- Skills learned
- Challenges overcome
- Feedback incorporated

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Full year covered
- [ ] Accomplishments are specific
- [ ] Impact is quantified where possible
- [ ] Praise included
- [ ] Themes are clear
- [ ] No fabrication
- [ ] Format matches company template
