## Task Breakdown Rules

### Turn Vague into Concrete
| Vague | Concrete |
|-------|----------|
| "Deal with insurance" | 1. Find policy number 2. Call member services 3. Ask specific questions |
| "Sort out taxes" | 1. Gather documents 2. Schedule with accountant 3. Prepare questions |
| "Renew thing" | 1. Find renewal deadline 2. Check if online 3. Complete renewal |

### Time Estimates
| Task Type | Typical Time |
|-----------|--------------|
| Quick online form | 5-10 min |
| Phone call (simple) | 10-15 min |
| Phone call (complex) | 20-30 min |
| Research + decision | 30-60 min |
| In-person errand | 30-60 min |

---

## Batching Rules

### Batch by Type
- Phone calls together
- Online tasks together
- Errands by location

### Batch by Energy
- High-energy: Complex calls, research
- Low-energy: Simple online forms
- Any-energy: Quick tasks

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] All items have specific actions
- [ ] Time estimates realistic
- [ ] Deadlines identified
- [ ] Similar tasks batched
- [ ] Schedule respects constraints
- [ ] Not overloaded
