## Status Definitions

### Active (Green)
- Contact within 30 days
- Relationship healthy
- No action needed

### Warm (Yellow)
- Contact 30-90 days ago
- Should reach out soon
- Low urgency

### Dormant (Orange)
- Contact 90-180 days ago
- Reconnection recommended
- Medium urgency

### At-Risk (Red)
- No contact 180+ days
- Relationship may be lost
- High priority if important

---

## Reconnection Rules

### Personalize Every Message
- Reference shared history
- Mention something specific
- Don't use templates verbatim

### Value-First Approach
- Share something useful
- Offer before asking
- Don't be transactional

### Context Matters
- Different cadence for different relationships
- Business vs. personal norms
- Cultural considerations
