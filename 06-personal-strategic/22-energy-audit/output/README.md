## What Appears Here

```
output/
├── energy-audit.md              # Full analysis

```

---

## Using the Outputs

### For Schedule Optimization
1. Review patterns
2. Choose 1-2 suggestions
3. Test for 2-4 weeks
4. Re-audit to measure

### For Boundary Setting
- Use data to justify schedule changes
- "My data shows meetings after 3pm drain productivity"

### For Energy Management
- Plan important work during peak times
- Schedule recovery after draining activities

---

## Iteration

Monthly audits show:
- Whether changes helped
- New patterns emerging
- Seasonal variations
