# Energy Audit — Setup Guide

## Choose Your Path

| If You Use... | Setup Time | What You Get |
|---------------|------------|--------------|
| Calendar | 2 min | Full time analysis |
| Energy Tracking | 0 min | Energy pattern analysis |
| Both | 2 min | Comprehensive audit |

---

## Option 1: Calendar Integration

### Google Calendar
```
Settings → Integrations → Google Calendar → Connect
```

### Apple Calendar
```
System Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendar → Enable
```

### What You Get
- Meeting time totals
- Time allocation by category
- Focus block identification
- Meeting pattern analysis

---

## Option 2: Energy Tracking

### Simple Method
Add to daily notes:
```
## Energy: 7/10
```

or just:
```
Good energy today
```

### Detailed Method
```markdown
- Morning: 8/10
- Afternoon: 5/10
- After meetings: 4/10
- After exercise: 8/10
```

## Verifying Setup

### Test Command
> "Test energy audit"

### Expected Result
```
✓ Calendar: [Connected/Not connected]
✓ Ready for energy analysis
```

---

## Energy Tracking Tips

### What to Note
- Overall daily energy (1-10)
- Energy after specific activities
- Sleep quality impact
- Exercise impact

### Keep It Simple
- Once per day is enough
- "High/Medium/Low" works
- Note unusual days
