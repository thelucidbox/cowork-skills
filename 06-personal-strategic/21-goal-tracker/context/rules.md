---

## Status Definitions

### On Track
- Progress ≥ expected for timeline
- Recent activity (within 2 weeks)
- No major blockers

### At Risk
- Progress behind expected
- OR no activity in 2-3 weeks
- Can still achieve with effort

### Unlikely
- Significantly behind
- OR no activity in 4+ weeks
- Would need major intervention

### Complete
- Target achieved
- Documented evidence

### Abandoned
- Explicitly dropped
- OR no activity in 8+ weeks with no explanation

---

## Trajectory Analysis

### Improving
- Recent progress accelerating
- Blockers being addressed
- Momentum building

### Stable
- Consistent progress
- On expected pace
- No significant changes

### Declining
- Progress slowing
- New blockers emerging
- Activity decreasing

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] All goals assessed
